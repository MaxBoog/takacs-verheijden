Who we are

Takacs & Verheijden

Takacs & Verheijden BV was started by Barbara Takacs and Ben Verheijden. They share more than twenty years of experience of training and coaching in higher education. Leadership in universities requires activities at three different levels:

  • Involving stakeholders navigating the ‘political arena’
  • Making clear strategic choices
  • implementing these choices operationally

There is never a shortage of wonderful plans and noble intentions between scientists and scholars. However, it often seems difficult to make choices and then to follow them through. In all our activities we aim to serve as an outside sounding board, a sparring partner, to help you find the next step in your leadership role.
